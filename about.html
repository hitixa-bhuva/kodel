About Kodel

Established in 2016, Kodel has emerged as a leading manufacturer of premium-quality leather products in India. With years of dedication and a passion for craftsmanship, we specialize in creating durable, stylish, and innovative leather solutions that cater to diverse industries including fashion, accessories, furniture, and automotive sectors.

Global standards that reflect our promise of excellence and integrity.

Our Mission & Vision

Driven by vision, guided by mission, and defined by values — we craft leather with purpose.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a trusted and innovative leather manufacturer, delivering products that combine quality, durability, and style.

Our Mission

  • To provide superior leather products through sustainable and ethical manufacturing practices.
  • To innovate continuously, ensuring our products meet international standards and customer expectations.
  • To build long-term relationships with clients by prioritizing quality, reliability, and service excellence.

Core Values

  • Expertise & Craftsmanship – Leveraging years of experience and deep knowledge to deliver unmatched leather solutions.
  • Quality & Durability – Ensuring every product meets strict quality standards for long-lasting performance.
  • Innovation – Continuously investing in designs, techniques, and sustainable practices to stay aligned with global trends.
  • Ethical & Sustainable Practices – Committing to eco-friendly processes and responsible sourcing.
  • Customer-Centricity – Building trust through tailored solutions, exceptional service, and timely delivery.
